Why You Should Choose Us

Experienced and Dedicated Teachers

Our teachers are local university graduates who have rich experience in teaching local students English. They have formulated localized strategies that can help second language learners learn English in a way they can understand better. Our teachers are also equipped with the latest technology-based strategies to teach students English with the most innovative and creative teaching strategies. Our teachers also use newspapers in the classroom to make sure the students can keep up with the times while learning English.

Tuition with a Passion

As more students are diagnosed with learning difficulties, we have introduced a new type of tuition class that accomodates their sensory needs and mastery level of the English language. The students can choose to have their classes either at home or at our premise on a one-to-one basis for better focus and optimum noise reduction.
